About Us

Welcome to iBeta, your local internet Café with great delicacies. I place where you can work with free
access to technology. We focus on making your experiance one of the best you'll ever have. With variety
of nutritional food and beverages and different devices. The Café started as a start up for Web development
Project, later on expanding to greater limits. Founded by Ikhmat and co-founded by Josh.

A company started in early March of 2022. Where other bussiness were growing at fast paces, no matter what
age. The project had started as a project then started to expand with the support of their teachers and ppers.
